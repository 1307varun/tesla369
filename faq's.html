LED Light

How can I install the Emergency Lights? +
Emergency lights are supposed to be installed with the help of professional support. These lights are recommended to be installed with an independent source of power supply for them to stay active especially during any power failure, emergency smoke breakout, etc
How Often am i supposed to replace (or) get the lights serviced? +
These Emergency lights do not need to be serviced that often. Although it is advised, one should fully discharge the light every three months just to ensure an improved battery-life.
After Charging the light fully, how long does it work for? +
It depends on the wattage and backup capacity of a specific model that is chosen by our client. Although, minimum backup range is for 1- 2 hours.
What is the Warranty and Guarantee of Emergency Lights?What all is covered under the Warranty and Guarantee? +
Warranty and guarantee of Emergency Lights is 1 year. Everything is covered under manufacturing defects, not under any breakage defects.
What are the Do's and Dont's of Emergency Lights? +
- Do's
• Charge the unit for 24 hours before first use.
• Repairs should be carried out only by qualified persons.
• Components authorized only by the company should be used.
- Dont's
• Do not expose to extreme heat or direct sunlight for longer period of time.
• Never store the unit in a discharged condition, to avoid any damage.

Yes, there is. We do provide spares and accessories.

SELF Illumination

What are the steps to install the autoglow Signages? +
- Autoglo Signages come with adhesive tape on their backs. One must just simply pull the tape. The signage is then ready to be stuck on the wall which is supposed to have a plain surface. If the surface happens to be uneven or rough, one can drill holes into the edges of the Signage and later fix it with screws.
How long does the glow last for? +
- Glow lasts for > 12 hours
How long does the Signages need to be exposed to the natural / artificial ligts to glow? +
- As these Signages come with high intensity of photoluminescent properties, minimum of 5 minute of exposure to light (natural/artificial) is ample to make the Signages glow.
What is the life span of the sheets?what is the guarantee and Warranty? +
- Life of the rigid sheet (indoors) is about 10 years, while that of aluminum sheet (outdoors) is 3 - 4 years. Warranty and guarantee period is 1 year.
Is there any Professional service for Installation? +
- We do not provide any professional service at the moment. Although, we would happily assist and guide you or the contractor regarding the same.
